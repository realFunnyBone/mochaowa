So, what else can you do?

Mocha is jam-packed with great features to help you stay in touch with friends, family and co-workers, share your thoughts and find the information you need.

You can use it on a computer or get Mocha on your mobile so you can keep in touch on the move, whatever you’re up to. Mocha works on a wide range of mobiles, plus on devices like the PlayStation® Portable (PSP®).

Just for starters you can instant message (IM) anyone on your contact list or even use group IM to chat with lots of people at the same time. You could hold a conference call to organise a get-together and then find business recommendations™ to search for the perfect venue to hold it.

Finding friends

If you use Microsoft Outlook®, Outlook Express®, or you have an email account with Yahoo! then it’s a simple task to import contacts and get them all onto your Mocha. If they don’t have Mocha yet you can send them an invite to download it – then you can talk for free (and maybe you’ll even get a nice bouquet of flowers and a chocolate bar from them to say thanks for getting them onto Mocha? We’d like to think that’s in order).

Got friends on MySpace? Get them to download Mocha or MySpaceIM with Mocha. Then add them to your Mocha contact list and you’ve got even more people to talk to for free. Simply search for their MySpaceIM name.

Adding someone to your contact list is easy. But you can also add people who are not yet on Mocha.
